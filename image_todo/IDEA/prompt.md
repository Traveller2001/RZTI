# IDEA - 点子喷泉

## 人格简介
想法比论文多十倍

## 角色形象描述
角色双臂张开，头向后仰，从头顶喷涌出数十个灯泡，像喷泉/间歇泉一样向上喷发。表情极度兴奋，眼睛睁得大大的，嘴巴张开呈惊喜状。但在脚下：散落着完全空白的纸张，上面什么都没写。形成鲜明对比——头顶是爆炸性的创意，脚下却是空空如也的产出。角色穿着学术休闲风格的衬衫和牛仔裤，整体姿态充满激情和能量。

## 生成 Prompt

### Style Prefix (所有图片统一使用)
Low-poly geometric paper-craft character illustration. Cube-shaped head with minimal facial features (simple dot eyes, small line mouth). Flat shading, soft pastel body colors, clean white background. No outlines, no shadows. Vector-like rendering, centered composition. Single character, full body visible. Style reference: origami/papercraft toy figure aesthetic.

### Character Prompt
A low-poly geometric papercraft character standing with both arms spread wide open, head tilted back looking upward. From the flat top surface of the cube-shaped head, dozens of small geometric lightbulb shapes are erupting upward like a fountain or geyser, spreading outward in an arc. The lightbulbs are in warm yellow and golden tones, glowing softly. The character has an ecstatic expression: dot eyes wide open, small mouth in an excited O shape. Wearing a casual lavender button-up shirt with rolled sleeves and dark blue pants. At the character's feet on the ground: several completely blank white sheets of paper scattered around, pristine and empty with nothing written on them. Strong visual contrast between the explosive burst of ideas above and the barren empty output below. Warm yellow and soft lavender color palette. Joyful yet ironic mood.

### Negative Prompt
realistic, photographic, anime, manga, 3D render, dark background, complex background, multiple characters, text, watermark, signature, blurry, low quality
