# TALK - 学术交际花

## 人格简介
你认识的人比你读的论文还多

## 角色形象描述
角色站在圆圈的中心，周围环绕着4-5个较小的人物，都在向中心倾斜倾听。双手都很活跃——一只手拿着扇形展开的名片，另一只手举着手机。嘴巴大张正在说话中，表情生动活泼。头顶上方飘浮着对话气泡图标。穿着时尚的商务休闲西装外套。社交蝴蝶般的能量，热情洋溢，是全场焦点。

## 生成 Prompt

### Style Prefix (所有图片统一使用)
Low-poly geometric paper-craft character illustration. Cube-shaped head with minimal facial features (simple dot eyes, small line mouth). Flat shading, soft pastel body colors, clean white background. No outlines, no shadows. Vector-like rendering, centered composition. Single character, full body visible. Style reference: origami/papercraft toy figure aesthetic.

### Character Prompt
A low-poly geometric papercraft character standing at the center of the composition, surrounded by 4-5 smaller, simpler geometric figures arranged in a circle around them, all leaning inward attentively. The main character is notably larger and more detailed than the surrounding figures. One hand holds a fan of small rectangular business cards spread out like playing cards. The other hand holds up a small geometric smartphone. The mouth is a wide open curved line mid-conversation, dot eyes bright and animated. Above the character's head, 2-3 small geometric speech bubble icons float upward. Wearing a smart-casual coral/salmon colored blazer over a white shirt, with dark pants. The surrounding smaller figures are in muted, simpler tones (light grays and soft blues) to keep focus on the central character. Warm coral, peach, and cream color palette. Lively, sociable, charismatic energy radiating from the center.

### Negative Prompt
realistic, photographic, anime, manga, 3D render, dark background, complex background, multiple characters, text, watermark, signature, blurry, low quality
