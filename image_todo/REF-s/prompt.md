# REF-s - 文献仓鼠

## 人格简介
"Zotero里3000篇，读完的30篇" —— 疯狂收集文献却永远读不完的囤积者。

## 角色形象描述
角色被困在一圈由学术论文和期刊堆成的高高的圆形围墙里。只有头部和两只举起的手露在纸墙上方。表情既不堪重负又带着一丝满足感。脸颊鼓鼓的，像仓鼠一样。两只手各抓着一篇不同的论文。整体传达出被文献淹没却乐在其中的矛盾感。

## 生成 Prompt

### Style Prefix (所有图片统一使用)
Low-poly geometric paper-craft character illustration. Cube-shaped head with minimal facial features (simple dot eyes, small line mouth). Flat shading, soft pastel body colors, clean white background. No outlines, no shadows. Vector-like rendering, centered composition. Single character, full body visible. Style reference: origami/papercraft toy figure aesthetic.

### Character Prompt
A cube-headed character buried inside a tall circular wall made of stacked academic papers, journals, and documents. The paper wall forms a cylindrical fortress around the character, reaching up to chin level. Only the character's cube head and two raised hands are visible above the wall of papers. The papers are in various soft colors — cream, white, light yellow, pale blue — stacked in a messy but tall ring. Each raised hand clutches a different paper document. The character's cheeks are puffed out like a hamster storing food, creating small rounded bumps on the sides of the cube face. The dot eyes show a mix of being overwhelmed and slightly pleased — perhaps slightly upward-curved. The mouth is a small wobbly line suggesting both stress and satisfaction. Color palette: warm creams, whites, soft yellows for papers, soft peach for character skin. Mood: overwhelmed hoarder who secretly enjoys the chaos.

### Negative Prompt
realistic, photographic, anime, manga, 3D render, dark background, complex background, multiple characters, text, watermark, signature, blurry, low quality
