# REVIEW - 审稿狂魔

## 人格简介
自己的论文不写，别人的论文看得很仔细

## 角色形象描述
角色一只手高举着别人的论文，论文上布满了密密麻麻的红色批注和叉号标记。另一只手像武器一样挥舞着一支粗粗的红笔。戴着红色镜片的阅读眼镜。表情锐利、挑剔、审视，一只眉毛挑起。在身后的桌上，自己的空白手稿被遗忘在一旁。整体气质是严格犀利的评审者，对别人的工作火眼金睛，对自己的工作则视而不见。

## 生成 Prompt

### Style Prefix (所有图片统一使用)
Low-poly geometric paper-craft character illustration. Cube-shaped head with minimal facial features (simple dot eyes, small line mouth). Flat shading, soft pastel body colors, clean white background. No outlines, no shadows. Vector-like rendering, centered composition. Single character, full body visible. Style reference: origami/papercraft toy figure aesthetic.

### Character Prompt
A low-poly geometric papercraft character standing in a critical reviewing pose. One hand held up high, gripping a geometric paper document that is visibly covered in red marks: small red X symbols, red underlines, and red scribble annotations all over it. The other hand brandishes a thick geometric red pen like a sword, pointing it forward assertively. The cube head wears small geometric reading glasses with red-tinted lenses. Expression is sharp and scrutinizing: one dot eye slightly narrower than the other suggesting a raised eyebrow, small tight disapproving line mouth. Behind the character on a small desk surface, a single blank white paper manuscript sits completely untouched and forgotten. The character wears a dark burgundy vest over a white shirt, looking scholarly and stern. Color palette: bold red accents from the pen and markings, deep burgundy, crisp white paper contrast. Critical, meticulous, judgmental academic reviewer energy.

### Negative Prompt
realistic, photographic, anime, manga, 3D render, dark background, complex background, multiple characters, text, watermark, signature, blurry, low quality
