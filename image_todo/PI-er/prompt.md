# PI-er - 未来PI

## 人格简介
"你不是在开组会就是在去开组会的路上" —— 天生的领导者，以成为PI为终极目标的学术野心家。

## 角色形象描述
角色自信地站在一块白板前，白板上画满了流程图、框图和各种学术图表。穿着一件整洁的西装外套/blazer，显得专业而有权威感。一只手握着白板笔，另一只手做出指向白板的手势，仿佛正在讲解。头顶漂浮着一顶小金冠，暗示其"未来老板"的身份。站姿稳健、充满自信和掌控感。

## 生成 Prompt

### Style Prefix (所有图片统一使用)
Low-poly geometric paper-craft character illustration. Cube-shaped head with minimal facial features (simple dot eyes, small line mouth). Flat shading, soft pastel body colors, clean white background. No outlines, no shadows. Vector-like rendering, centered composition. Single character, full body visible. Style reference: origami/papercraft toy figure aesthetic.

### Character Prompt
A cube-headed character standing confidently in front of a large whiteboard. The whiteboard behind the character is covered with colorful diagrams, flowcharts, boxes connected by arrows, and small chart sketches in blue, red, and green marker colors. The character wears a neat dark navy blazer/suit jacket over a light blue shirt. One hand holds a red whiteboard marker raised near shoulder height, the other hand extends outward gesturing toward the whiteboard in a presenting motion. A small golden crown floats just above the cube head, slightly tilted at a jaunty angle. The character's stance is wide and authoritative — feet apart, chest forward. The facial expression shows confidence with simple dot eyes looking straight ahead and a small satisfied line mouth. Color palette: navy blue blazer, light blue shirt, golden crown, colorful whiteboard markings. Mood: leadership, ambition, authority.

### Negative Prompt
realistic, photographic, anime, manga, 3D render, dark background, complex background, multiple characters, text, watermark, signature, blurry, low quality
