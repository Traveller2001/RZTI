# STAR - 学术新星

## 人格简介
"好奇、敢冲、还能写，老天爷赏饭吃" —— 天赋与努力兼具的学术新星，前途一片光明。

## 角色形象描述
角色高高站在由堆叠的论文构成的阶梯/基座上。一只手像举火炬一样高举着一支笔，另一只手捧着一座金色奖杯/奖牌。身体散发着柔和的光芒。头部周围有闪烁的星星形状。表情自信、仰望天空。身后飘着一件像披风一样的实验白大褂。整体传达出冉冉升起的新星气质和无限潜力。

## 生成 Prompt

### Style Prefix (所有图片统一使用)
Low-poly geometric paper-craft character illustration. Cube-shaped head with minimal facial features (simple dot eyes, small line mouth). Flat shading, soft pastel body colors, clean white background. No outlines, no shadows. Vector-like rendering, centered composition. Single character, full body visible. Style reference: origami/papercraft toy figure aesthetic.

### Character Prompt
A cube-headed character standing tall and triumphant on top of a staircase or tiered pedestal made of stacked academic papers and documents, with 3-4 ascending steps. The character stands at the very top. One hand holds a pen raised high above the head like a torch, pointing upward toward the sky. The other hand holds a golden trophy cup at waist level — a classic chalice-shaped award in bright gold color. The character's body emits a soft warm golden aura or glow that radiates outward. Around the cube head, 4-5 small twinkling star shapes sparkle in yellow and white, like a constellation of achievement. The character wears a white lab coat that flows behind like a superhero's cape, caught in an imaginary wind, billowing dramatically. Underneath is a simple light yellow shirt. The facial expression shows confidence and aspiration — dot eyes looking slightly upward, and the line mouth forms a determined, proud smile. The stair-pedestal papers are in cream and white tones. Color palette: white cape-like lab coat, gold trophy, yellow stars, golden aura, cream paper stairs. Mood: rising star, brilliant potential, triumphant ascent, boundless future.

### Negative Prompt
realistic, photographic, anime, manga, 3D render, dark background, complex background, multiple characters, text, watermark, signature, blurry, low quality
