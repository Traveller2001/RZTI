# MONK - 学术苦行僧

## 人格简介
"十年磨一剑，磨完发现刀柄断了" —— 以苦修精神对待学术的长期主义者。

## 角色形象描述
角色盘腿而坐，呈冥想姿势。穿着一件像僧袍一样的飘逸学术袍，金色/藏红花色。膝上放着一份手稿/论文。双手合十做祈祷/冥想状。面容安详但眉头微蹙，陷入深思。头部后方有一圈淡淡的圆形光晕。整体传达出苦修般的学术精神和深沉的思考。

## 生成 Prompt

### Style Prefix (所有图片统一使用)
Low-poly geometric paper-craft character illustration. Cube-shaped head with minimal facial features (simple dot eyes, small line mouth). Flat shading, soft pastel body colors, clean white background. No outlines, no shadows. Vector-like rendering, centered composition. Single character, full body visible. Style reference: origami/papercraft toy figure aesthetic.

### Character Prompt
A cube-headed character sitting cross-legged in a classic meditation pose on the ground. The character wears a flowing, draped robe in warm golden/saffron color, reminiscent of a monk's robe, with soft fabric folds falling around the seated figure. A manuscript or academic paper rests flat on the character's lap, visible between the crossed legs, in cream/white color with faint line markings. Both hands are pressed together in front of the chest in a prayer/meditation gesture, fingers pointing upward. The facial expression is serene but with slightly furrowed brow lines above the dot eyes, suggesting deep contemplation. The dot eyes are half-closed in meditation. The line mouth is neutral and peaceful. Behind the cube head, a soft circular halo or glow radiates outward in a pale warm yellow/golden tone, giving a saintly aura. Color palette: golden/saffron robe, cream manuscript, warm yellow halo, soft peach skin. Mood: devoted asceticism, profound contemplation, spiritual dedication to scholarship.

### Negative Prompt
realistic, photographic, anime, manga, 3D render, dark background, complex background, multiple characters, text, watermark, signature, blurry, low quality
