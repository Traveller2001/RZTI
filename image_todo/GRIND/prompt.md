# GRIND - 卷王

## 人格简介
实验室的灯为你而亮

## 角色形象描述
角色笔直地坐在书桌前，极度专注。桌上的台灯发出温暖的光照在身上。桌面上堆满了论文、图表和数据打印稿。身后的窗户显示着漆黑的夜空和月亮。眼睛因疲惫而微微发红，但依然燃烧着坚定的决心。穿着实验室白大褂。墙上时钟显示晚上11点。整体氛围是深夜独自奋斗的坚毅与执着。

## 生成 Prompt

### Style Prefix (所有图片统一使用)
Low-poly geometric paper-craft character illustration. Cube-shaped head with minimal facial features (simple dot eyes, small line mouth). Flat shading, soft pastel body colors, clean white background. No outlines, no shadows. Vector-like rendering, centered composition. Single character, full body visible. Style reference: origami/papercraft toy figure aesthetic.

### Character Prompt
A low-poly geometric papercraft character sitting rigidly upright at a geometric desk, intensely focused forward. A small geometric desk lamp on the desk casts a warm golden glow onto the character. The desk surface is piled high with stacked papers, small chart graphics, and data printout sheets in organized chaos. Behind the character, a simple rectangular window shape shows a dark indigo night sky with a small geometric crescent moon. The character's dot eyes have tiny reddish accents suggesting exhaustion, but the overall expression is one of fierce determination: slightly furrowed brow area, tight small line mouth. Wearing a white lab coat over a simple blue shirt. On the wall beside the window, a small round clock showing 11:00 PM. The warm lamp light contrasts with the cool dark night outside. Color palette: warm amber from the lamp, cool indigo night, crisp white lab coat. Atmosphere of relentless late-night dedication and grinding perseverance.

### Negative Prompt
realistic, photographic, anime, manga, 3D render, dark background, complex background, multiple characters, text, watermark, signature, blurry, low quality
