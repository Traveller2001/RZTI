# CHILL - 佛系学者

## 人格简介
"论文？缘分到了自然会有的" —— 随遇而安的学术佛系青年，一切顺其自然。

## 角色形象描述
角色大幅度向后仰靠在办公椅上，双脚翘在桌面上。一只手懒洋洋地端着一小杯茶。桌上有一份没动过的论文和一台合着的笔记本电脑。眼睛闭着，挂着平和满足的微笑。头顶漂浮着一朵小云或"禅"的符号。全身肢体语言极度放松。整体传达出一种对学术毫无焦虑、顺其自然的佛系态度。

## 生成 Prompt

### Style Prefix (所有图片统一使用)
Low-poly geometric paper-craft character illustration. Cube-shaped head with minimal facial features (simple dot eyes, small line mouth). Flat shading, soft pastel body colors, clean white background. No outlines, no shadows. Vector-like rendering, centered composition. Single character, full body visible. Style reference: origami/papercraft toy figure aesthetic.

### Character Prompt
A cube-headed character leaning far back in a simple office chair, the body at a steep reclined angle. Both feet are propped up on the edge of a desk in front, legs crossed casually at the ankles. One hand lazily holds a small traditional tea cup (small round cup in soft celadon green) near the chest. On the desk surface sits an untouched white paper document and a closed gray laptop, both clearly ignored. The character's dot eyes are closed — represented by two small curved lines, like peaceful sleeping eyes. The line mouth curves into a serene, content little smile. Above the cube head, a small fluffy white cloud floats, with a tiny zen/meditation circle symbol inside it. The character wears a comfortable-looking soft mint green cardigan over a white shirt. The office chair is a simple gray swivel chair. The overall body language is extremely relaxed and carefree. Color palette: mint green cardigan, celadon tea cup, gray chair and laptop, white paper, peaceful whites and greens. Mood: ultimate tranquility, zero stress, zen-like acceptance.

### Negative Prompt
realistic, photographic, anime, manga, 3D render, dark background, complex background, multiple characters, text, watermark, signature, blurry, low quality
