# TANK - 学术坦克

## 人格简介
"拒稿？那是审稿人的损失" —— 百折不挠的学术铁人，拒稿只会让他更强。

## 角色形象描述
角色挺拔站立，双臂交叉抱在胸前。穿着一件厚实的马甲/护甲式外套，胸前有一个盾牌徽章。表情坚毅如磐石，嘴角微微上扬露出自信的微笑。脚下踩着一堆被揉皱的拒稿信和信封。站姿稳如泰山，不可撼动。整体传达出面对拒稿毫不退缩的钢铁意志。

## 生成 Prompt

### Style Prefix (所有图片统一使用)
Low-poly geometric paper-craft character illustration. Cube-shaped head with minimal facial features (simple dot eyes, small line mouth). Flat shading, soft pastel body colors, clean white background. No outlines, no shadows. Vector-like rendering, centered composition. Single character, full body visible. Style reference: origami/papercraft toy figure aesthetic.

### Character Prompt
A cube-headed character standing tall and upright with both arms crossed firmly over the chest. The character wears a sturdy-looking vest or armor-like jacket in a deep steel blue/gray color, with a small golden shield emblem on the chest center. Underneath is a simple dark shirt. The stance is wide and rock-solid — feet planted firmly apart. The facial expression shows unwavering determination: dot eyes looking straight ahead with intensity, and a slight confident smirk (small upward curve on one side of the line mouth). Below the character's feet is a scattered pile of crumpled papers, envelopes, and rejection letters in white and light pink tones — clearly stepped on and conquered. The character's overall posture radiates immovability and resilience. Color palette: steel blue/gray vest, gold shield emblem, white/pink crumpled papers below. Mood: indestructible confidence, defiant resilience.

### Negative Prompt
realistic, photographic, anime, manga, 3D render, dark background, complex background, multiple characters, text, watermark, signature, blurry, low quality
