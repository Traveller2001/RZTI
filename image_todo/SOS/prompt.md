# SOS - 学术求生者

## 人格简介
"我是不是不适合做科研.jpg" —— 在学术海洋中挣扎求存的迷茫灵魂。

## 角色形象描述
角色正在沉入一片由论文组成的"海洋"中，只有头部和一只举起的手露在纸面上方。举起的手中挥舞着一面小白旗。眼睛睁得大大的充满惊慌，有汗珠飞出。论文像海浪一样漂浮翻涌在周围。整个表情传达出痛苦和被淹没的感觉。

## 生成 Prompt

### Style Prefix (所有图片统一使用)
Low-poly geometric paper-craft character illustration. Cube-shaped head with minimal facial features (simple dot eyes, small line mouth). Flat shading, soft pastel body colors, clean white background. No outlines, no shadows. Vector-like rendering, centered composition. Single character, full body visible. Style reference: origami/papercraft toy figure aesthetic.

### Character Prompt
A cube-headed character sinking into a sea made entirely of papers and documents. The paper sea forms wave-like shapes across the lower two-thirds of the image. Only the character's cube head and one raised hand are visible above the paper surface — the body is submerged below. The raised hand waves a small white surrender flag on a thin stick. The character's dot eyes are wide open in panic — enlarged dots with tiny highlight spots showing fear. Two or three small blue sweat drops fly off the sides of the head. The line mouth is a wavy distressed shape. Individual papers float and curl around the character like ocean waves, some partially folded, in white, cream, and light gray tones. The composition shows the character at center, half-submerged, with paper waves spreading outward. Color palette: white and cream papers, soft peach character, white flag, blue sweat drops. Mood: overwhelmed, drowning, desperate call for help.

### Negative Prompt
realistic, photographic, anime, manga, 3D render, dark background, complex background, multiple characters, text, watermark, signature, blurry, low quality
