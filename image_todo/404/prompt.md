## 角色形象描述
角色的身体是刻意做成故障/像素化的效果——由散落的马赛克方块组成，正在崩解或重组中。人物轮廓仅勉强能辨认出是一个人形。头部位置上方漂浮着大大的"404"文字/标志。方块形的头部已经部分解构成漂浮的几何碎片。整体神秘、迷幻。配色方案以灰色为主，偶尔点缀故障风格的紫色和青色色斑。

## 生成 Prompt

### Style Prefix (所有图片统一使用)
Low-poly geometric paper-craft character illustration. Cube-shaped head with minimal facial features (simple dot eyes, small line mouth). Flat shading, soft pastel body colors, clean white background. No outlines, no shadows. Vector-like rendering, centered composition. Single character, full body visible. Style reference: origami/papercraft toy figure aesthetic.

### Character Prompt
A low-poly geometric papercraft character that is deliberately glitchy and deconstructing. The body is made of scattered mosaic-like geometric blocks that are visibly falling apart, drifting away from the main form, and rearranging in mid-air. The overall silhouette is barely recognizable as a humanoid figure - fragments floating in approximate person shape. Above where the cube head should be, a large bold geometric "404" text floats prominently. The cube head itself is partially deconstructed: the top half has broken into several floating geometric fragments that hover above and around the head position, with visible gaps between the pieces. Some facial feature dots are displaced - one eye dot floating slightly away from the face surface. The character has no clear expression due to the fragmentation - mysterious and unknowable. The main color scheme is neutral grays and silver tones for most of the body blocks, with occasional accent blocks in glitch-like electric purple and bright cyan scattered randomly throughout the figure, as if digital artifacts or corrupted pixels. Some blocks at the edges of the figure are mid-flight, dissolving into the white background. Enigmatic, undefined, error-state aesthetic. Digital glitch meets papercraft.

### Negative Prompt
realistic, photographic, anime, manga, 3D render, dark background, complex background, multiple characters, text, watermark, signature, blurry, low quality
