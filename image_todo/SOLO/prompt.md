# SOLO - 独行侠

## 人格简介
我的最佳合作者是我自己

## 角色形象描述
角色独自站立，双手插在深色长风衣的口袋里。身体微微侧转，背对着远处背景中一群模糊的、正在聊天的小人。表情冷静、疏离但充满自信。风微微吹动风衣的衣角。一只手臂下夹着一台笔记本电脑或一篇论文。散发出独狼般的气质，有尊严的孤独感。整体氛围沉稳、独立、不需要任何人。

## 生成 Prompt

### Style Prefix (所有图片统一使用)
Low-poly geometric paper-craft character illustration. Cube-shaped head with minimal facial features (simple dot eyes, small line mouth). Flat shading, soft pastel body colors, clean white background. No outlines, no shadows. Vector-like rendering, centered composition. Single character, full body visible. Style reference: origami/papercraft toy figure aesthetic.

### Character Prompt
A low-poly geometric papercraft character standing alone in a cool, confident pose. Both hands tucked into the pockets of a long dark navy trench coat that reaches below the knees. Body turned at a three-quarter angle, slightly looking away. In the far background, very small and slightly faded/blurred, a cluster of 3-4 tiny geometric figures are grouped together chatting (indicated by small speech marks). The main character stands distinctly separated from them. Expression is calm and self-assured: neutral dot eyes, straight small line mouth, composed demeanor. One arm has a small geometric laptop or folded paper tucked underneath. The coat has subtle geometric folds, with one edge slightly lifted as if caught by a gentle breeze. Color palette: deep navy coat, cool gray tones, muted slate blue. Dignified solitary atmosphere, lone wolf energy. The character is clearly the focal point, standing tall and independent.

### Negative Prompt
realistic, photographic, anime, manga, 3D render, dark background, complex background, multiple characters, text, watermark, signature, blurry, low quality
