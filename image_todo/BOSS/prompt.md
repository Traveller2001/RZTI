# BOSS - 天生导师

## 人格简介
"组会上你总是那个画饼的人" —— 天生的导师和引路人，擅长描绘宏伟蓝图。

## 角色形象描述
角色站在一个讲台/讲桌上，位置略微抬高。一只手握着激光笔。身后是一个投影屏幕，上面显示着一个饼图（"画饼"的双关语）。前方有3-4个更小的方头小人坐着作为听众仰望着。面带自信温暖的微笑。穿着有领衬衫。整体传达出一种导师风范和领导魅力。

## 生成 Prompt

### Style Prefix (所有图片统一使用)
Low-poly geometric paper-craft character illustration. Cube-shaped head with minimal facial features (simple dot eyes, small line mouth). Flat shading, soft pastel body colors, clean white background. No outlines, no shadows. Vector-like rendering, centered composition. Single character, full body visible. Style reference: origami/papercraft toy figure aesthetic.

### Character Prompt
A cube-headed character standing slightly elevated on a small podium or raised platform, positioned at center. One hand holds a thin red laser pointer, extended toward a projection screen behind them. The projection screen is a simple white rectangle mounted behind and above, displaying a colorful pie chart with 4-5 segments in soft pastel colors (pink, blue, yellow, green, orange) — a visual pun on the Chinese expression for making empty promises. In the foreground at the bottom of the image, 3-4 much smaller cube-headed figures sit in a row as audience members, seen from behind, their tiny cube heads looking upward at the main character. The main character wears a neat light blue collared dress shirt, properly buttoned. The facial expression shows a confident, warm smile — dot eyes with a gentle, kind look, and the line mouth curves upward in a genuine smile. Color palette: light blue shirt, colorful pie chart, soft pastel audience figures in various muted colors. Mood: charismatic mentor, inspiring leadership, warm authority.

### Negative Prompt
realistic, photographic, anime, manga, 3D render, dark background, complex background, text, watermark, signature, blurry, low quality
