# RUSH - DDL战士

## 人格简介
"还有72小时？充足了" —— 在deadline的肾上腺素中如鱼得水的极限选手。

## 角色形象描述
角色呈动感的奔跑姿势，身体前倾带有速度线。一只胳膊下夹着笔记本电脑边跑边冲。身后一个长了腿的巨大闹钟正在追赶。表情兴奋、激动（不是害怕）——享受这种紧迫感。头发被风吹向后方。穿着休闲服装。整体传达出在DDL压力下反而充满活力的状态。

## 生成 Prompt

### Style Prefix (所有图片统一使用)
Low-poly geometric paper-craft character illustration. Cube-shaped head with minimal facial features (simple dot eyes, small line mouth). Flat shading, soft pastel body colors, clean white background. No outlines, no shadows. Vector-like rendering, centered composition. Single character, full body visible. Style reference: origami/papercraft toy figure aesthetic.

### Character Prompt
A cube-headed character in a dynamic running pose, body leaning forward at an angle with one leg stretched behind and one forward, conveying speed and urgency. One arm tucks a small gray laptop under it while running. The other arm pumps forward in a sprinting motion. Behind the character, a giant anthropomorphic alarm clock with two small legs is chasing — the clock is round, red/coral colored, with a classic bell on top and simple clock face showing nearly midnight. Two or three horizontal speed lines trail behind the running character. The character's hair is blown straight back by wind, in dark brown color. The facial expression is excited and thrilled — dot eyes are bright and energized, the line mouth is open in a wide excited grin. NOT a scared expression — this character loves the rush. The character wears a casual coral/orange t-shirt and simple dark pants. Color palette: coral/orange shirt, gray laptop, red alarm clock, dynamic energy. Mood: exhilarating rush, adrenaline-fueled excitement, thriving under pressure.

### Negative Prompt
realistic, photographic, anime, manga, 3D render, dark background, complex background, multiple characters, text, watermark, signature, blurry, low quality
