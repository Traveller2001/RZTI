# PIVOT - 转向大师

## 人格简介
换方向比换衣服还勤

## 角色形象描述
角色站在十字路口，面前有一根高高的路标柱。路标上有4个箭头指向不同方向，每个箭头上标有代表不同研究领域的小图标（DNA双螺旋、电路板、大脑、原子）。角色正在旋转/转身中，身体动态扭转。地面上有轮胎刹车的痕迹。表情兴奋、充满冒险精神。一只脚抬离地面，正在转向的动作中。整体传达出频繁切换研究方向的动感和随性。

## 生成 Prompt

### Style Prefix (所有图片统一使用)
Low-poly geometric paper-craft character illustration. Cube-shaped head with minimal facial features (simple dot eyes, small line mouth). Flat shading, soft pastel body colors, clean white background. No outlines, no shadows. Vector-like rendering, centered composition. Single character, full body visible. Style reference: origami/papercraft toy figure aesthetic.

### Character Prompt
A low-poly geometric papercraft character standing at a crossroads, mid-spin in a dynamic turning motion. Next to the character, a tall geometric signpost with 4 directional arrow signs pointing in different directions. Each arrow has a tiny icon: a DNA double helix shape, a small circuit board pattern, a brain silhouette, and an atom symbol. The character's body is dynamically twisted mid-turn: torso facing one direction, legs another, one foot lifted off the ground in the turning motion. On the ground beneath the character, curved skid mark lines suggest rapid direction changes. Expression is excited and adventurous: wide dot eyes, open grinning line mouth. Wearing a colorful patchwork-style jacket with different colored geometric panels (suggesting many different fields), casual jeans, and running shoes ready for quick pivots. Color palette: varied and colorful with each signpost arrow in a different pastel tone (green for DNA, blue for circuit, pink for brain, orange for atom). Energetic, restless, direction-changing momentum.

### Negative Prompt
realistic, photographic, anime, manga, 3D render, dark background, complex background, multiple characters, text, watermark, signature, blurry, low quality
