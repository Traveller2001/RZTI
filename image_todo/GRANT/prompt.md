## 角色形象描述
角色穿着冒险家/猎人装备——头戴插着羽毛的探险帽（Safari帽）。一只手拿着蝴蝶网，网中已经捕获了美元符号($)和硬币符号。另一只手得意地高举着一份经费申请书。表情精明、老练、胸有成竹地微笑着。站姿呈胜利者姿态，充满征服感。整体形象是一个精于猎取科研经费的老猎手。

## 生成 Prompt

### Style Prefix (所有图片统一使用)
Low-poly geometric paper-craft character illustration. Cube-shaped head with minimal facial features (simple dot eyes, small line mouth). Flat shading, soft pastel body colors, clean white background. No outlines, no shadows. Vector-like rendering, centered composition. Single character, full body visible. Style reference: origami/papercraft toy figure aesthetic.

### Character Prompt
A low-poly geometric papercraft character dressed in an adventurer outfit standing in a triumphant victorious pose. Wearing a geometric safari/explorer hat on top of the cube head, with a small angular feather tucked into the hat band. One hand holds up a geometric butterfly net, inside the net are several small geometric dollar signs ($) and circular coin symbols caught like butterflies. The other hand raised triumphantly holds up a geometric document labeled as a grant proposal (small rectangular paper with lines on it). Expression is sharp, shrewd, and confidently smiling: dot eyes with a calculating glint, small satisfied smirk mouth. Wearing an explorer-style khaki vest with many small pockets over a forest green shirt, with sturdy brown pants and boots. Standing with legs apart in a power pose, chest slightly puffed out. Color palette: earthy khaki, forest green, warm gold from the captured dollar signs. Confident hunter-gatherer energy, resourceful and victorious mood.

### Negative Prompt
realistic, photographic, anime, manga, 3D render, dark background, complex background, multiple characters, text, watermark, signature, blurry, low quality
