# CAFE - 咖啡续命

## 人格简介
"DDL是第一生产力，咖啡是第二" —— 靠咖啡因维持生命的深夜科研工作者。

## 角色形象描述
角色一只手举着一个夸张大的咖啡杯（比头还大），另一只手在笔记本电脑上疯狂打字。眼睛半睁半闭，下面挂着浓重的黑眼圈，尽显疲惫。头发凌乱如刚起床。穿着一件皱巴巴的连帽衫。背景中有一个时钟显示凌晨3:00。整体传达出一种深夜赶工、靠咖啡续命的科研日常。

## 生成 Prompt

### Style Prefix (所有图片统一使用)
Low-poly geometric paper-craft character illustration. Cube-shaped head with minimal facial features (simple dot eyes, small line mouth). Flat shading, soft pastel body colors, clean white background. No outlines, no shadows. Vector-like rendering, centered composition. Single character, full body visible. Style reference: origami/papercraft toy figure aesthetic.

### Character Prompt
A cube-headed character sitting at a desk. One hand holds a comically oversized coffee mug that is bigger than the character's entire head — the mug is a warm brown/terracotta color with visible steam wisps rising from it. The other hand is on a small laptop keyboard, fingers spread in a frantic typing motion. The character's dot eyes are half-closed, drooping with sleepiness. Prominent dark semicircle bags under the eyes in a grayish-purple tone. Hair is messy bed-head style, sticking up in random directions, in dark brown. The character wears a wrinkled, slouchy sage green hoodie with the hood down. In the background, slightly behind and to the right, a simple round wall clock shows 3:00 AM (hands pointing to 12 and 3). The laptop is a simple gray rectangle shape. Color palette: sage green hoodie, brown coffee mug, gray laptop, warm tones. Mood: exhausted but persevering, late-night grind.

### Negative Prompt
realistic, photographic, anime, manga, 3D render, dark background, complex background, multiple characters, text, watermark, signature, blurry, low quality
