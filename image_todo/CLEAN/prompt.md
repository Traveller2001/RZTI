# CLEAN - 数据洁癖

## 人格简介
你的代码注释比代码还多

## 角色形象描述
角色戴着洁白无瑕的手套。一只手举着放大镜对着一个小屏幕/显示器，屏幕上显示着排列完美整齐的代码行。另一只手精细地调整屏幕位置。身后是一个整理得一丝不苟的书架/文件架，上面摆满了按颜色分类的文件夹。表情严肃、一丝不苟、高度专注。周围的一切都完美对称、整洁有序。实验室白大褂一尘不染、熨烫平整。整体传达出极致的秩序感和对细节的偏执追求。

### Style Prefix (所有图片统一使用)

Flat shading, soft pastel body colors, clean white background. No outlines, no shadows. Vector-like rendering, centered composition. Single character, full body visible. Style reference: origami/papercraft toy figure aesthetic.

### Character Prompt
A low-poly geometric papercraft character standing in a precise, careful posture. Both hands wearing pristine white geometric gloves. One gloved hand holds up a small geometric magnifying glass, examining a small geometric monitor/screen that displays perfectly aligned horizontal lines representing clean code. The other gloved hand delicately adjusts the screen's position with fingertip precision. Behind the character, a geometric bookshelf/file rack is immaculately organized with color-coded geometric folders arranged in a perfect rainbow gradient: red, orange, yellow, green, blue, purple - all perfectly evenly spaced. Expression is serious and meticulous: focused dot eyes looking through the magnifying glass, small precise straight line mouth showing concentration. Wearing a spotless, perfectly pressed white lab coat that is crisp and wrinkle-free, over a light blue perfectly buttoned shirt. Everything in the scene is symmetrical and orderly. Color palette: clean whites, precise light blues, organized rainbow accents on the folders. Perfectionist, obsessively tidy, detail-oriented atmosphere.

### Negative Prompt
