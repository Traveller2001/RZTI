# PAPER - 论文机器

## 人格简介
"你不发论文，论文发你" —— 高效冷静的论文量产者，把学术写作变成流水线作业。

## 角色形象描述
角色坐在一张桌子后方，面前堆着一摞比自己还高的论文稿件。双手各握一支笔，同时在两张不同的稿纸上书写。面部表情淡定从容，毫无压力感。穿着简洁的白色衬衫，干练利落。桌面上散落着各种论文草稿和便签纸，其中一张论文上有一个小小的"accepted"印章标记。整体传达出一种不慌不忙、批量生产论文的机器感。

## 生成 Prompt

### Style Prefix (所有图片统一使用)
Low-poly geometric paper-craft character illustration. Cube-shaped head with minimal facial features (simple dot eyes, small line mouth). Flat shading, soft pastel body colors, clean white background. No outlines, no shadows. Vector-like rendering, centered composition. Single character, full body visible. Style reference: origami/papercraft toy figure aesthetic.

### Character Prompt
A cube-headed character sitting behind a wooden desk piled with a towering stack of academic papers that extends well above the character's head. Both hands each hold a pen, actively writing on two separate sheets of paper simultaneously. The character wears a clean, simple white collared shirt. The facial expression is calm and completely unfazed — simple dot eyes looking down at the work, flat small line mouth showing composure. Papers and loose sheets are scattered across the desk surface. One visible paper near the front has a small red "ACCEPTED" stamp on it. The paper stack is in soft cream and white tones. The desk is a warm light brown. The character's skin is a soft peach tone. Overall mood: productive, mechanical efficiency, serene focus.

### Negative Prompt
realistic, photographic, anime, manga, 3D render, dark background, complex background, multiple characters, text, watermark, signature, blurry, low quality
