## 人格简介
"别人还在调参数，我已经换了三个方向" —— 不走寻常路的学术颠覆者，擅长另辟蹊径。

## 角色形象描述
角色穿着深色连帽衫且帽子戴着。蹲着身子，正用螺丝刀拆解/改装一个大型机械齿轮装置。脸上露出狡黠的坏笑，一只眼睛微微眯起。周围散落着各种工具和零件。姿态充满动感，暗示着快速行动和灵活变通。

The character's face shows a mischievous sly grin — one dot eye is slightly smaller/squinted than the other, and the line mouth curves up on one side in a smirk. Scattered around the character on the ground are small tools (wrench, bolts, screws, small gears) in metallic colors. The pose is energetic and low to the ground, suggesting quick nimble movement. Color palette: dark charcoal hoodie, metallic gray and bronze gears, scattered silver tools. Mood: cunning, resourceful, rebellious innovation.
