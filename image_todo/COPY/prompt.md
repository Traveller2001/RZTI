# COPY - 跟风侠

## 人格简介
什么热做什么，从不踩空

## 角色形象描述
角色正踩在一道由热门论文堆叠而成的浪花上冲浪，姿态动感十足。一只手举着放大镜，正在仔细审视一篇标有火焰/热门图标的论文。头发被风吹起，脸上带着兴奋的投机式咧嘴笑容。穿着运动休闲风格的衣服（连帽衫+运动裤）。身后有几张打着红叉的旧论文正在飘落，代表已经过时的研究方向。整体构图呈动态冲浪姿态，身体微微前倾，充满冒险和追逐潮流的能量。

## 生成 Prompt

### Style Prefix (所有图片统一使用)
Low-poly geometric paper-craft character illustration. Cube-shaped head with minimal facial features (simple dot eyes, small line mouth). Flat shading, soft pastel body colors, clean white background. No outlines, no shadows. Vector-like rendering, centered composition. Single character, full body visible. Style reference: origami/papercraft toy figure aesthetic.

### Character Prompt
A low-poly geometric papercraft character in a dynamic surfing pose, riding on a large wave made of stacked academic papers and documents. The wave curls upward with visible paper sheets forming its shape. One hand raised high holding a small magnifying glass, examining a paper marked with a bright orange fire/flame icon. The other arm stretched out for balance. Hair pieces (geometric angular chunks) blown backward by wind. Face shows an excited, opportunistic wide grin (simple curved line mouth, dot eyes squinting with excitement). Wearing a sporty casual outfit: a teal hoodie and light gray jogger pants, small sneakers. Behind the character, several papers marked with red X symbols are tumbling and falling away in the wind. Body leaning forward dynamically on the paper wave. Soft pastel teal and warm orange color palette. Energetic, trend-chasing adventurous mood.

### Negative Prompt
realistic, photographic, anime, manga, 3D render, dark background, complex background, multiple characters, text, watermark, signature, blurry, low quality
