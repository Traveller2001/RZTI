# SLIDE - 摸鱼大师

## 人格简介
用最少的力气拿到学位

## 角色形象描述
角色深深地瘫在办公椅上，几乎呈水平状态。一只手偷偷在桌子下面玩手机。桌上的电脑屏幕上打开着一篇PDF论文作为掩护。另一只手懒洋洋地拿着游戏手柄。脸上挂着狡猾而满足的坏笑。脚翘在桌上。太阳镜推到额头上。整体姿态呈现出最大程度的放松状态，散发着偷懒摸鱼的快乐。

## 生成 Prompt

### Style Prefix (所有图片统一使用)
Low-poly geometric paper-craft character illustration. Cube-shaped head with minimal facial features (simple dot eyes, small line mouth). Flat shading, soft pastel body colors, clean white background. No outlines, no shadows. Vector-like rendering, centered composition. Single character, full body visible. Style reference: origami/papercraft toy figure aesthetic.

### Character Prompt
A low-poly geometric papercraft character slouched extremely low in a geometric office chair, body almost horizontal, legs stretched far out. One hand sneakily holds a small geometric smartphone below desk level, partially hidden. On the desk, a geometric monitor screen displays a simple rectangle representing a PDF paper as camouflage. The other hand loosely holds a small geometric game controller resting on the lap. Face shows a sly, content smirk: dot eyes half-closed in a knowing expression, small curved smug line mouth. Feet propped up on the edge of the desk. Small geometric sunglasses pushed up onto the forehead of the cube head. The character wears a casual mint green t-shirt and khaki shorts. The chair is tilted back at a precarious angle. Color palette: relaxed mint green, warm khaki, light cream. Maximum chill, slacker energy, clever laziness vibe. Everything about the pose radiates effortless minimal-effort comfort.

### Negative Prompt
realistic, photographic, anime, manga, 3D render, dark background, complex background, multiple characters, text, watermark, signature, blurry, low quality
