# BURN - 学术燃尽

## 人格简介
曾经热爱科研，现在热爱下班

## 角色形象描述
角色瘫坐在椅子上，双肩下垂。头顶像一根已经熄灭的蜡烛——一缕细细的烟从头顶升起，火焰已经熄灭。一只无力的手中松松地垂着一支笔。眼睛睁着但完全空洞——千码凝视。角色整体颜色灰暗/去饱和度处理。实验室白大褂也是暗淡褪色的。整体传达出学术倦怠、精神耗尽、曾经的热情已经完全燃尽的状态。

## 生成 Prompt

### Style Prefix (所有图片统一使用)
Low-poly geometric paper-craft character illustration. Cube-shaped head with minimal facial features (simple dot eyes, small line mouth). Flat shading, soft pastel body colors, clean white background. No outlines, no shadows. Vector-like rendering, centered composition. Single character, full body visible. Style reference: origami/papercraft toy figure aesthetic.

### Character Prompt
A low-poly geometric papercraft character sitting slumped in a chair, shoulders drooped heavily downward, entire posture conveying exhaustion and defeat. From the flat top of the cube-shaped head, a thin wispy geometric smoke trail rises upward, as if the head was a candle that has just been extinguished - the flame is gone, only a fading smoke wisp remains. One hand hangs limply at the side with a small geometric pen dangling loosely from the fingers, about to fall. The dot eyes are open but completely vacant and hollow - a thousand-yard stare looking at nothing. The small line mouth is flat and expressionless. The entire character is rendered in deliberately desaturated, greyed-out muted tones - the lab coat is a dull faded gray-white instead of bright white, the clothes underneath are washed-out pale blue-gray. Everything about the character looks drained of color and energy. The chair is slightly reclined from the weight of the slump. Color palette: muted grays, desaturated blues, faded whites, with the smoke wisp being the only slightly visible element rising above. Exhausted, burnt-out, hollow emptiness mood.

### Negative Prompt
realistic, photographic, anime, manga, 3D render, dark background, complex background, multiple characters, text, watermark, signature, blurry, low quality
