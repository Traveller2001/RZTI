# NERD - 学术宅

## 人格简介
"别跟我说impact factor，我在想一个有趣的问题" —— 纯粹的知识追求者，沉浸在学术世界里无法自拔。

## 角色形象描述
角色盘腿坐在地板上，怀里紧紧抱着一本几乎和自己一样大的摊开的书本。戴着厚厚的圆框眼镜，头发凌乱蓬松不修边幅。眼睛睁得大大的，闪烁着好奇和兴奋的光芒。头顶上方漂浮着一个小灯泡，象征灵感闪现。穿着一件宽大松垮的毛衣，显得整个人缩在衣服里。整体传达出一种对知识的纯粹热爱和孩童般的好奇心。

## 生成 Prompt

### Style Prefix (所有图片统一使用)
Low-poly geometric paper-craft character illustration. Cube-shaped head with minimal facial features (simple dot eyes, small line mouth). Flat shading, soft pastel body colors, clean white background. No outlines, no shadows. Vector-like rendering, centered composition. Single character, full body visible. Style reference: origami/papercraft toy figure aesthetic.

### Character Prompt
A cube-headed character sitting cross-legged on the floor, hugging an oversized open book that is nearly as large as the character itself. The book is open wide with visible page lines, in soft cream color. The character wears thick round glasses (circular frames in dark gray). Hair is messy, unkempt, sticking out in multiple directions in dark brown color. The dot eyes are extra wide and sparkling with curiosity — two bright highlights in each eye to show excitement. A small glowing yellow lightbulb floats above the character's head, representing a moment of inspiration. The character wears a baggy, oversized soft lavender/purple sweater with sleeves extending past the hands. Sitting posture is cozy and absorbed. The overall color palette is soft purples, creams, and warm browns. Mood: pure intellectual curiosity, childlike wonder.

### Negative Prompt
realistic, photographic, anime, manga, 3D render, dark background, complex background, multiple characters, text, watermark, signature, blurry, low quality
