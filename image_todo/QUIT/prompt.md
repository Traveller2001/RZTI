# QUIT - 摆烂者 (隐藏人格)

## 人格简介
科研？不熟，我先去考个公

## 角色形象描述
角色正从实验室门口走出来，面朝观众方向前进。背着一个塞满书本的背包。一只手拿着一本公务员考试备考书。回头望向身后的实验室，表情复杂——嘴角带着释然的微笑，但眼神中有一丝不舍。身后的实验室门上挂着一个小小的"LAB"标志。脚下的路通向前方的日出/明亮的地平线。整体传达出告别学术、走向新生活的转折感。

## 生成 Prompt

### Style Prefix (所有图片统一使用)
Low-poly geometric paper-craft character illustration. Cube-shaped head with minimal facial features (simple dot eyes, small line mouth). Flat shading, soft pastel body colors, clean white background. No outlines, no shadows. Vector-like rendering, centered composition. Single character, full body visible. Style reference: origami/papercraft toy figure aesthetic.

### Character Prompt
A low-poly geometric papercraft character walking forward toward the viewer, body in mid-stride. Looking back over one shoulder toward a geometric laboratory door visible behind them. The door is a simple rectangular shape with a small sign reading "LAB" above it. Wearing a geometric backpack that is visibly stuffed and bulging with books peeking out from the top. One hand holds a small geometric book with visible text lines on the cover representing a civil service exam prep book. The expression is complex and nuanced: a small relieved smile on the line mouth, but the dot eyes looking backward carry a hint of wistfulness and nostalgia. Wearing casual everyday clothes - a warm orange sweater and comfortable pants, dressed for a new chapter. At the character's feet, a simple geometric path extends forward toward the bottom of the frame, leading to a soft warm sunrise glow - gentle yellow and peach gradient suggesting a bright new horizon ahead. The lab door behind is slightly darker and cooler in tone. Color palette: warm hopeful orange and sunrise peach ahead, cooler muted tones for the lab behind. Bittersweet transition mood, leaving the old behind and stepping into something new.

### Negative Prompt
realistic, photographic, anime, manga, 3D render, dark background, complex background, multiple characters, text, watermark, signature, blurry, low quality
